Sisonke

Sisonke is a mock-up project for a South African colloquial dictionary. The goal of the dictionary is to educate the public.The visual design concept features an illustration collage of individuals from the general public, expressing and sharing commonly used slangs within South African society. This approach aims to capture the essence of local language and promote diversity.
